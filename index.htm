SCI FI delicacies.

Appetizers:

Heart of Targ

A traditional Klingon dish, a Targ heart (Klingon boars) is believed to bring courage to the warrior who eats it.

Hasperat

A well known spicy Bajoran food, it is cooked in a special brine which scorches the tongue if done right.

Algae Puffs

A local baked Enaran delicacy.

Fortress du Fromage


Sliced cheese served with a cracker assortment, stabbed with toothpicks that are gold and silver star-tipped wands

Official Hogwarts Brie


A small round of brie cheese, wrapped in puff pastry, brushed with butter, with a crispy crust.

Parchment Scrolls

Meat and cheese rolled in flat bread to replicate real magical scrolls!

Main Courses:

Luhvian Quail

A small bird of similar to the Earth quail and served in truffle sauce, it makes a fine starter to any multi-course dinner.

Seltin Pate

Traditionally made with wood fungus from the Seltin planet, this pate is a delicious appetizer for lunch or parties.

Quidditch Players Pie

Quidditch players need a hearty meal like shepherd's pie to energize them for the match. As Fleur Delacour says, "Zis 'eavy 'ogwarts food."

Beef, Lamb, and Guinness Stew

Served at the Leaky Cauldron, beef, lamb and Guinness stew is originally in a bread bowl but can be made like a pot pie if you prefer pastry dough.

Sides:

Plomeek Soup

Plomeek soup is a traditional dish on the Vulcan home world. Taking a page from the mostly vegetarian Vulcans, the Earth version is vegan-friendly.

Parthas a la Yuta

A delicacy prepared by Yuta: the parthas plant, a green vegetable with fleshy roots .

Yalasat (stuffed cabbage)

Of the Torothan homeworld, they have Yalasat, a type of cactus. This dish is nicely flavored by apples and dried fruit.

Yorkshire Pudding

Made from batter consisting of eggs, flour, and milk. The dish is usually served with roast meat and gravy.

Frogs Eye Salad

Acinidi pepe (small round pasta) mixed with lentils, mushrooms and peas.

Pumpkin Fries

Pumpkin steak cut fries, fried to crispy perfection!

Beverages:

Trixian Bubble Juice

Trixian bubble juice was an alcoholic pink beverage served at Quark's on Deep Space Nine. It is a carbonated beverage made with fruit nectar.

Raktajino

The favorite drink of the swing shift and Starfleet captains alike. While a Klingon drink, it seems to be almost more popular with many other races than with the Klingons on Deep Space Nine. Made with ground Turkish coffee, cardamom, and cream.

Butterbeer

Hot or cold, butterbeer, made with real butter and beer, is a staple to take the edge off.

Pumpkin Juice

Muggles drink OJ, but wizards subside off of pumpkin juice morning, noon, and night.

Desserts:

Rokeg Blood Pie

An ancient Klingon recipe, Rokeg blood pie is served during their 'Day of Honor' holiday. In direct contrast to the Vulcans, this dish is decidedly not vegan friendly.

Jumba Stick

Made from the sap of the Bajoran jumja tree, these confections are notable for their insane sweetness.

Acid Pops

Dipped supersour lollipops in honey, and rolled in Pop Rocks for crackling effect.

Treacle Tart

Harry's favorite dessert features a shortbread crust and golden syrup filling.
